A Tribute For Swami Vivekananda
That Will Inspire Youth

The revolutionary leader of Vedanta philosophy, the devoted disciple of Sri Ramakrishna, the founder of Ramakrishna Mission – Swami Vivekananda was a regal, majestic figure of commanding presence, vast learning symbol, and deep insight. Only at the age of 30, he created a stir at the World’s Parliament of Religions in Chicago in 1893.

Let’s focusing on some motivational and inspirational quotes from him who showed us the true definition of life.

1. Don’t stop till the goal is reached

It is said that falling down is how we grow, but staying down is how we die. Strive for the never -ending progress until our dreams don’t scare us thinking that they are big enough!

2. Great Things Never Came From Comfort Zone

The biggest risk is not taking any risk. In the world that changing really quickly, the only strategy that is guaranteed to fail is not taking risks. Always say yes to the new adventure.

3. Trust the power that lies within You!

Life is waiting for each of us to open up to it. The wisdom and intelligence of Universe is yours’ to use. There is an inner light hidden inside each and every people. Discover the light that can shine through all circumstances, is all about the journey of life!

4. Take every experience as Learning

Our soul is greater than every wining and most importantly every losing. Being the unique self is always better than being the copy of others. At the rest, everyone’s life is different and we all have the different set of question paper to answer.

5. Have faith on yourself!

It is said that everything you can cross, except faith. Faith is like a bird that sings when the dawn is still dark. Faith is the light of our hearts when our eyes see the darkness. Maybe faith doesn’t make things easy, but it makes them possible.
